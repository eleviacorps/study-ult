## What This Chapter Covers

1. **Electric Charge** - Origin, types, properties (quantization, additivity, conservation)
2. **Coulomb's Law** - Force between point charges, permittivity constant
3. **Superposition Principle** - Forces in multi-charge systems
4. **[[Electric Field]]** - Definition, calculation, physical significance
5. **[[Electric Field]] Lines** - Mapping fields, rules of field lines
6. **[[Electric Flux]]** - Concept and calculation
7. **[[Electric Dipole]]** - Field due to dipole, dipole moment
8. **Dipole in External Field** - Torque, potential energy
9. **Gauss's Law** - Derivation and applications
10. **[[Continuous Charge Distributions]]** - Linear, surface, volume densities
