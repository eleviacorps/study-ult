# Electrostatics Formula Sheet

This sheet links formulas to [[Gauss Law]], [[Electric Potential]], and [[Capacitance]] for quick revision.

| Concept | Formula | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Coulomb force | $F = kq_1q_2/r^2$ | Two point charges |
| Point field | $E = kq/r^2$ | Radial field |
| Potential | $V = kq/r$ | Scalar addition |
| Capacitance | $C = Q/V$ | Charge storage |

- Q:: What is the relation between field and potential? A:: Electric field is the negative gradient of potential, written as E = -dV/dr in one dimension.
- MCQ:: Capacitance depends primarily on: Options:: Geometry and dielectric | Stored charge only | Potential only | Current Answer:: Geometry and dielectric
