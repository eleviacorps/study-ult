# Calculus Core

Calculus connects [[Limits]], [[Differentiation]], [[Definite Integration]], and [[Differential Equations]].

## Core Intuition

Differentiation measures local rate of change. Integration measures accumulated change.

$$\frac{d}{dx}x^n = nx^{n-1}$$

$$\int_a^b f(x)dx$$

- Q:: What is the geometric meaning of a definite integral? A:: It gives signed area under the curve over the given interval.
- MCQ:: Derivative of sin x is: Options:: cos x | -cos x | tan x | -sin x Answer:: cos x
