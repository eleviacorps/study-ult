# Chemical Bonding Core

Chemical bonding connects [[VSEPR]], [[Hybridisation]], [[Molecular Orbital Theory]], and [[Ionic Character]].

## JEE Lens

- Draw Lewis structure first.
- Count lone pairs before predicting geometry.
- Compare formal charge and resonance stability.
- Use bond order for stability and magnetic behavior.

- Q:: What does bond order predict? A:: Higher bond order generally means shorter bond length and higher bond strength.
- MCQ:: Shape of NH3 according to VSEPR is: Options:: Trigonal pyramidal | Trigonal planar | Linear | Tetrahedral Answer:: Trigonal pyramidal
